About Me

Hi, My name is Alex Tayou and I a data enthusiast. I hold a Bachelor's degree
in Business Administration fron SUNY Canton College of Technology and a Master's degree
in Data Science from Mercyhurst University.

data sets: for me it is one big puzzle I want to solve. Finding patterns nobody else sees
is the challenge for me.

I have been working as a data analyst for about three years now with experience in sales,
marketing, retail and cyber security. I have assisted over 15+ small and medium size companies
translate complex questions into understandable insights through data.
